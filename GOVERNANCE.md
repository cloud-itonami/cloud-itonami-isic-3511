# Governance

cloud-itonami-isic-3511 follows open-source governance with transparent
decision-making documented in ADRs.

Scope: Compliance recordkeeping -- safety-inspection-record logging,
licensing-submission drafting, fuel-custody-record logging,
community-benefit-report drafting, safety-concern escalation.

Out-of-scope, permanently: control-rod operation, reactor-trip/scram
decisions, criticality-safety determinations, radiological-release/dose
authorization, containment-integrity overrides, emergency-evacuation
orders, fuel-loading/refueling sequencing, security-force response
decisions.

See README.md for the complete CRITICAL scope exclusions and the
absolute, un-overridable live-actuation-request block.
